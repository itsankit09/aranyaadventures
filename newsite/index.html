ABOUT

Aranya Adventures - In the Lap Of Nature

As it is rightly said, Jungle is a journey not a destination. Everything revamps with the first second in jungle, the trees get dense, the animals turn wild, the air gets musky, the water turns fresh, the sound gets soothing, the sky becomes clear and the mind becomes serene.

The beauty of Madhya Pradesh lies in loads of forest reserves spread across the length and breadth of the region. Many such awe-inspiring attractions are in the deep forest on the huge reservoir of Indira Sagar dam of Khandwa district.

Camping in this forest Reserve has to be an unparalleled experience, right? Aranya Adventures is a pioneer in successfully running camping expeditions in such tranquil forests since last few years. The tents here are styled in a hermit fashion and are designed to give you total aloof time. This camp is your go-to place if you are looking for a chance to introspect your inner self. The food served here is entirely organic. Apart from detoxifying, you can undertake Jungle Safari, Trekking, Boating and many activities.
